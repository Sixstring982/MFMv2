Detailed Description

template<class CC>
class MFM::Element_DBar< CC >

A diagonal bar-forming class, with symmetry.

Bar grid positions in an event window 

              -4    -2 -1  0 1  2
              /     /  /  /  / /

-4 . . . . . x . x . x / / / / / / -3 . . . . . . . . . . / / / / / / -2 . . |x| . |x| . x . . / / / / / / -1 . . . . . . . . . / / / / / / 0 . . x . C . |x| . . / / / / / / 1 . . . . . . . . . / / / / / 2 x . x . x . . / / / / 3 . . . . . . . . . / / / / 4 x . x . x . . . .

Each bar atom knows how big the grid is supposed to be (GetMax()), where within that grid it is supposed to be (GetPos()), and what symmetry it is using (GetSym()).

Class: (C1) Name is Element_DBar (C2) Type is 0xdba2
